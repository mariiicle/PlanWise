Subscription FAQ

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The remaining value of your current subscription will be prorated and applied to your new plan.

What happens when my subscription ends?

When your subscription ends, you'll be downgraded to the free plan. Your projects will remain accessible, but premium features will be disabled.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.
