Quick Start Guide

Create Account

Launch your design journey by creating a free account in less than hour. Our streamlined registration process requires minimal information while offering extensive customization options. Once registered, you'll have immediate access to your personalized workspace where you can set preferences for units, grid layouts, and interface appearance to match your workflow.

Many users find it helpful to complete their profile with professional information and specialty areas, as this enables better collaboration and networking within the PlanWise community. Don't forget to explore the accessibility settings if you have specific visibility or interface needs.

Customize Vicinity

The vicinity map is your starting point for spatial context. Begin by customizing own map. Our precision boundary tools allow you to define exact project areas by using simulated objects. You can then layer contextual elements like nearby buildings, vegetation, and infrastructure to understand your site's relationship to its surroundings.

The map interface supports multiple annotation types—pins, areas, paths, and text—each with customizable appearances. As you progress, you can toggle different analysis layers to visualize factors like traffic flow, noise levels, and visibility from key vantage points.

Site Analysis

Our site analysis tools transform raw data into actionable design insights. The solar path simulator shows sun movement across all seasons, helping you optimize building orientation and window placement. The integrated wind pattern analyzer uses computational fluid dynamics to model airflow around existing structures, essential for natural ventilation strategies.

Matrix Diagram

The matrix diagram tool revolutionizes how you visualize relationships between program spaces. Starting with a project-specific template (residential, commercial, educational, etc.), you'll define spaces and begin mapping their connections. Our relationship strength indicators use a five-point scale from "essential adjacency" to "separation required," with color-coding that makes patterns immediately apparent.

The intelligent system can suggest common relationships based on your project type, saving time while allowing complete customization. As you build your matrix, you'll see it dynamically update with statistical insights about circulation efficiency and functional clustering. This data-driven approach helps justify design decisions and creates a solid foundation for your bubble diagram development.

Bubble Diagram

Transform abstract concepts into spatial arrangements with our intuitive bubble diagram tool. Simply drag space "bubbles" onto the canvas where their size automatically reflects your program areas. Connections between spaces appear dynamically based on your matrix relationships, with line thickness indicating connection importance. Color-coding distinguishes between public, private, service, and circulation spaces at a glance.

The multi-level layering system lets you organize spaces by floor or zone while maintaining visual connections between levels. As you arrange bubbles, real-time feedback highlights optimal and problematic adjacencies. When satisfied with your layout, export to vector formats for further development in design software or direct inclusion in presentations.

Zoning

The zoning module helps you organize spaces into functional groups with sophisticated parameters. Begin by defining zone types (public/private, wet/dry, loud/quiet) and assigning priority levels to each criterion. The system's spatial optimization algorithm suggests efficient arrangements while respecting your design intent. Our conflict detection technology immediately identifies when zones violate established relationships.

The efficiency analysis tools measure circulation distances, spatial overlap, and program compatibility, quantifying the effectiveness of your layout decisions. Save successful zoning schemes as templates for future projects, building a personal library of proven spatial strategies. This systematic approach ensures your designs are both creative and functionally sound.
