PlanWise simplifies early design for architecture students and professionals with intuitive tools for site analysis, matrix diagrams, and bubble diagrams.

The Ultimate Tool for Architectural Initial Planning

From site analysis to zoning, from matrix diagrams to bubble diagrams.
Your all-in-one tool for conceptualizing early architectural designs.


Site Analysis

Analyze environmental factors including sun path, wind direction, noise sources, and circulation patterns. Generate comprehensive site analysis diagrams with customizable layers and export options.

Zoning & Regulations

Map and analyze the surrounding context including transportation, amenities, landmarks, and infrastructure. Understand how your site connects to the broader urban fabric.

Matrix Diagram

Create relationship matrices between different functional spaces to optimize connections and proximities. Automated generation based on your input with customizable parameters.

Bubble Diagram

Transform your matrix diagrams into spatial bubble diagrams showing relationships and approximate sizes. Drag, resize, and adjust to explore different spatial organizations easily.

One Site for Early Design Planning

Streamline your early design process with intuitive planning and visualization tools.


Vicinity Mapping

Easily visualize zoning laws, height restrictions, and building coverage to ensure compliance.

3D Massing Study

Convert your 2D planning into simple 3D volumetric studies. Explore building form, orientation, and shadow studies with easy-to-use modeling tools.
